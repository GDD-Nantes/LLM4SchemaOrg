###### OBITUARY

# Franklin Delano Gallaher

###### May 26, 1945 - September 14, 2022

IN THE CARE OF

[

## Whitten Monelison Chapel

](/funeral-homes/madison-heights-va/whitten-monelison-chapel/4895)

Franklin Delano Gallaher, 77, of Goode, passed away peacefully at his
residence on Wednesday, September 14, 2022. Born on May 26, 1945 in Lynchburg,
he was a veteran of the United States Navy. He was preceded in death by his
wife, Patricia B. Gallaher. He is survived by his son, Lee Gallaher (Ariel);
step-children Steve Fetherolf, Jenny Fetherolf and Melissa Wagstaff; and two
granddaughters, Alyssandra Gallaher and Giovanna Gallaher. A graveside service
will be conducted at 11:00 a.m. on Wednesday, September 21, 2022 at Briarwood
Memorial Gardens. Military Honors will be provided by American Legion Post 16.
