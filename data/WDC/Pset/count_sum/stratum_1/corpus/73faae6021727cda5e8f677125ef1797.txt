# Referral Sources

l

#### Physicians

Working with Pediatricians and Specialists, we partner with clinical leaders
to improve patient outcomes and improve the lives of families and caregivers.

i

#### Hospitals

Knowing that patients will receive quality, consistent care allows us to work
with Hospital Case Management/Discharge Planners throughout the state.



#### Community Partners

Partnering with Community Resources allows us to be active participants in the
day-to-day lives of our patients and to support the needs of the Pediatric
population.

## Why Refer to Spark?

Z

#### Quality of Care

Spark employs highly skilled RNs, LPNs and CNAs. Each patient receives a
specialized Plan of Care tailored to their specific needs. By working with
children and their families over the long term we are able to see them develop
and grow



#### Scope of Services

We provide both Nursing and Therapy (PT/OT/SLP) services throughout the state
of Florida. In addition, we have the ability to take care of our patients
either in one of our centers or their home if in Broward or Dade counties



#### Medical Directors

Highly accredited Medical Directors are responsible for the administration of
care at each of our centers. These are Pediatric MDs who ensure quality,
compliance and liaise with the local communities that we serve

w

#### Communication

We provide continuous feedback to referral sources and families on the
progress of our patients. We view both our referral sources and families as
key partners in the administration of care to our Pediatric patients
