![2019 Kennedy Center Honors
](https://assets2.cbsnewsstatic.com/hub/i/r/2019/12/16/670ad2f7-bac9-48a1-98db-d665547c254b/thumbnail/620x413/884d9a107be17ee739ceb4e5f3a41c75/ap-19342155335291.jpg#)
AP

The 2019 honorees are: Actress Sally Field, singer Linda Ronstadt, disco-funk
band Earth Wind and Fire, conductor Michael Tilson Thomas and long-running
children's TV show "Sesame Street."

Front row from left, Susan Pompeo, 2019 Kennedy Center Honorees Michael Tilson
Thomas, Linda Ronstadt, Sally Field, Joan Ganz Cooney, Lloyd Morrisett and
Kennedy Center President Deborah F. Rutter, back row from left, Secretary of
State Mike Pompeo, 2019 Kennedy Center Honorees Philip Bailey, Verdine White,
Ralph Johnson, and characters from "Sesame Street," Abby Cadabby, Big Bird,
and Elmo, and Kennedy Center Chairman David M. Rubenstein, pose for a group
photo following the Kennedy Center Honors State Department Dinner on Saturday,
December 7, 2019, in Washington, D.C.
