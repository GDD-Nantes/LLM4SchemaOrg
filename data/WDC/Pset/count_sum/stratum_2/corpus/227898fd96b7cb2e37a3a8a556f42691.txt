## **LOCATION & NEIGHBOURHOOD**

The project Shapoorji Pallonji Joyville Sector 102 Gurgaon is located at a
strategic location of sector 102 which is not only known as a developing area
but also holds excellent connectivity with the rest of the city and nearby
cities which is the biggest positive for this project. There are all the
routine facilities available near this project which includes schools,
colleges, shopping malls, multiplexes, restaurants, and the market as well as
banks. Hence for the dwellers here, it can offer a high quality living with
all the facilities near the home only. Hence, if one wants to have the
comfortable life with the premium property, this property is the right option
to go for. It is a combination of quality living at a premium location which
can rarely be seen.

  * 23 km from ITC Grand Bharat

  * 35 minutes from major business and entertainment hub - The Cyber Hub

  * 38 minutes from Sikandarpur Metro Station

  * 47 minutes from Indira Gandhi International Airport

  * 56 minutes from GD Goenka University
