PayAngel

Send / Receive payment securely in Africa

About Project

PayAngel is a money transfer app developed for the African diaspora to easily make payments for goods and services in their country. Our products and services are designed to provide tailored payment solutions to individuals and businesses in the markets we serve. PayAngel is fast, reliable, secure and convenient, allowing customers to make online payments anytime and anywhere in their country as long as they have internet access.

Business Needs

The client wanted to develop an app that uses a digital money transfer solution within Africa. The main need for this money transfer service was to operate a decentralized platform that would work without a central authority. It is also a permanent solution for easy money transfer/exchange via mobile phone with full security.

How It Works

To execute our vision, we will offer innovative payment solutions with increased convenience, better exchange rates and provide an exceptional overall service with no hidden fees and charges. We will continue to leverage technology and welcome ideas that enhance our product offering to ensure that our customers and clients receive greater savings and continuous exceptional service.
