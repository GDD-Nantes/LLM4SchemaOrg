# QuickBooks Services

## R&D’s Bookkeeping Services Let You Focus On Your Customers

R&D’s Bookkeeping Services let you focus on your clients, core business, and
bottom line – providing a great return on investment.

## R&D’s Mission Is To Help Small Law Firms And Businesses With QuickBooks

We realize that some organizations may not feel comfortable outsourcing their
bookkeeping needs. So as Intuit-Certified Quickbooks ProAdvisors, we offer:

QuickBooks Setup - New installations and upgrades  
QuickBooks Conversion - Data transfers  
QuickBooks Training - Group sessions or private classes

As a member of the QuickBooks ProAdvisor Program, we are up-to-date with all
the current information and products available to our clients. So you can be
assured that you will receive the best possible QuickBooks service.
