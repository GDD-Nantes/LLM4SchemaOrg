The document pertains to a movie that is accessible in both auditory and visual modes. This means that the movie can be enjoyed by both hearing and visually impaired individuals. The movie has been equipped with certain accessibility features to enhance the viewing experience for individuals with disabilities. These features include audio descriptions and captions.

The movie provides sufficient access modes for different types of audiences. For instance, it provides closed captioning, which is a combination of textual and visual elements. This feature is particularly beneficial for individuals who are hard of hearing as it allows them to follow the dialogue and the action of the movie. 

Another access mode that the movie provides is audio description. This feature is primarily auditory and is designed to assist visually impaired individuals. The audio description provides a narration of the visual elements of the movie, such as the actions, characters, locations, costumes, and sets, which are not conveyed by the dialogue alone.

The movie also provides an accessibility summary. According to the summary, captions are provided in English. However, for short scenes in French, English subtitles are provided instead. This ensures that non-French speaking audiences can understand the dialogue and follow the storyline.
