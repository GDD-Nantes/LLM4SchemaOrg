This document pertains to a job posting for the position of a Software Engineer. The educational requirements for this role specify that the candidate must possess an Educational Occupational Credential, specifically a bachelor's degree. 

In terms of experience, the job posting outlines Occupational Experience Requirements. The candidate is expected to have a minimum of 60 months, or five years, of relevant experience in the field. 

However, it is important to note that the job posting also allows for experience in place of education. This means that even if a candidate does not meet the specified educational requirements, they may still be considered for the role if they have sufficient relevant experience. This flexibility in requirements ensures that the job posting is open to a wide range of potential candidates, not just those who have followed a traditional educational path.
