The US Small Business Administration has made a special announcement regarding a new Paycheck Protection Program for small businesses. This announcement was made on March 30, 2020, and the information provided will remain valid until April 24, 2020. 

The Paycheck Protection Program is a new loan initiative aimed at helping small businesses keep their workforce employed during the Coronavirus (COVID-19) crisis. This program falls under the category of Business Support, as defined by schema.org. 

The program is applicable to small businesses located within the United States. The US Small Business Administration, a government organization, is the provider of this service. 

For more information about the Paycheck Protection Program, you can visit the official website of the US Small Business Administration at https://www.sba.gov/funding-programs/loans/coronavirus-relief-options/paycheck-protection-program-ppp. 

This initiative is part of the government's benefits aimed at supporting small businesses during these challenging times. The administration encourages all eligible small businesses to take advantage of this opportunity to protect their workforce and ensure business continuity.
