The US Small Business Administration has made a special announcement regarding a new Paycheck Protection Program for small businesses. This announcement was made on March 30, 2020, and the information provided will remain valid until April 24, 2020. 

The Paycheck Protection Program is a new loan initiative aimed at helping small businesses keep their workforce employed during the ongoing Coronavirus (COVID-19) crisis. This program falls under the category of Business Support, as per the classification provided by Wikidata. 

The program is applicable to small businesses across the United States, as indicated by the spatial coverage of the announcement. The US Small Business Administration, a government organization, is the provider of this service. 

For more information about the Paycheck Protection Program, interested parties can visit the official website of the US Small Business Administration. The specific URL for the program is https://www.sba.gov/funding-programs/loans/coronavirus-relief-options/paycheck-protection-program-ppp. 

This initiative is a part of the government's benefits aimed at providing support to small businesses during these challenging times. The audience for this program is specifically small businesses that are looking for financial support to maintain their workforce amidst the pandemic.
